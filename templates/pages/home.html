Equity, One Payroll at a Time

    Welcome to Hummingbird. Built, designed, and developed by four college students at HackSC2020, Hummingbird levels the floor for everyone when it comes to knowing all of the details about payrolls across genders, race, and position titles. Everyone is entitled to a fair paycheck and we want to ensure that employees across the globe have easy and convenient access to the information that can reveal the inequality between payrolls and paychecks.
    Gaining accessibility to convoluted, complicated, and overwhelming employer-released payroll data has never been easier. With Hummingbird, we process, mark-up, and beautify the data that hundreds of employers across the globe release regarding their employees, genders, annual salaries, and much more. Hummingbird draws comparisions between these factors and draws out graphs, providing multiple perspectives at which to interpret the data. Embark upon a new era of ever increasing acccessibility, with Hummingbird at the front of it all.
